1. What Shifa is getting

This is not a template website — it is a complete custom e-commerce + Order Management System: a customer storefront (installable app, bilingual, SEO-ready) and a full staff back-office (orders, packing, courier dispatch, COD reconciliation, inventory, procurement, finance, GST invoicing, reports, CRM, audit). Comparable custom builds in the market are typically quoted at ₹1,50,000–₹3,00,000+. Our pricing below is kept deliberately lean to stay competitive with local Indore vendors while delivering far more capability.
